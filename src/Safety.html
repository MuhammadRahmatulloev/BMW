Uncompromised Safety: Driving the Future of Protection

At BMW, safety is not an option; it's a foundational principle. Our vehicles integrate cutting-edge active and passive systems, complemented by intuitive driver assistance technologies, designed to protect you and your passengers in every journey. We are constantly innovating to exceed global safety standards, ensuring every BMW delivers confidence alongside exhilarating performance.

Active Safety Features

Active Cruise Control with Stop & Go

Automatically adjusts your speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead, even in stop-and-go traffic.

Lane Keeping Assistant with Active Side Collision Protection

Helps prevent unintentional lane departures and intervenes with steering assistance to avoid potential side collisions.

Automatic Emergency Braking

Detects potential frontal collisions and applies brakes automatically to mitigate or prevent impact.

Parking Assistant Plus with Surround View

Simplifies parking with a 360-degree view of your vehicle's surroundings, ensuring precise maneuvering.

Adaptive LED Headlights

Illuminate the road dynamically, adjusting light distribution for optimal visibility around curves and in varying conditions.

Blind Spot Detection

Alerts you to vehicles in your blind spots with visual warnings in the exterior mirrors and haptic feedback.
