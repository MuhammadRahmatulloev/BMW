Uncompromised Safety: Driving the Future of Protection

At BMW, safety is not an option; it's a foundational principle. Our vehicles integrate cutting-edge active and passive systems, complemented by intuitive driver assistance technologies, designed to protect you and your passengers in every journey. We are constantly innovating to exceed global safety standards, ensuring every BMW delivers confidence alongside exhilarating performance.

Active Safety Features

Active Cruise Control with Stop & Go

Automatically adjusts your speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead, even in stop-and-go traffic.

Lane Keeping Assistant with Active Side Collision Protection

Helps prevent unintentional lane departures and intervenes with steering assistance to avoid potential side collisions.

Automatic Emergency Braking

Detects potential frontal collisions and applies brakes automatically to mitigate or prevent impact.

Parking Assistant Plus with Surround View

Simplifies parking with a 360-degree view of your vehicle's surroundings, ensuring precise maneuvering.

Adaptive LED Headlights

Illuminate the road dynamically, adjusting light distribution for optimal visibility around curves and in varying conditions.

Blind Spot Detection

Alerts you to vehicles in your blind spots with visual warnings in the exterior mirrors and haptic feedback.

Active Safety Features

Comprehensive Vibrate System

Multiple airbags strategically placed throughout the cabin provide optimal protection for occupants in a collision.

High-Strength Steel Chassis

Engineered with advanced materials and construction methods to create an incredibly rigid safety cell, absorbing crash energy effectively.

Active Front Headrests

Designed to move forward during a rear-end collision, reducing the risk of whiplash injuries.

Pyrotechnic Belt Tensioners

Instantly tighten seatbelts upon impact, securing occupants firmly in their seats before the full force of a collision.

Pedestrian Protection Systems

Includes an active bonnet that rises slightly upon impact, creating a deformation zone to reduce pedestrian injury severity.

Reinforced Side Impact Beams

Integrated into the doors, these beams distribute impact forces across the vehicle structure, enhancing side-on collision protection.

Rigorous Testing for Real-World Safety

Frontal Impact Assessment

Our vehicles endure severe frontal impact tests to ensure maximum occupant protection and structural integrity.

Side Impact Simulation

Simulating lateral collisions to optimize side curtain airbag deployment and body structure performance.

Rear Impact Analysis

Focused on preventing whiplash and maintaining fuel system integrity during rear-end collisions.

Rollover Protection Testing

Ensuring roof strength and occupant containment in the rare event of a rollover incident.

Our Unwavering Safety Philosophy

"The ultimate driving experience must always be the safest driving experience."

At BMW, safety is intrinsically linked to driving pleasure. Our engineers are driven by a holistic 'Safety 360' approach, which encompasses preventing accidents (active safety), protecting occupants if an accident is unavoidable (passive safety), and providing swift assistance after a collision. This commitment ensures that every innovation, from intelligent sensor systems to advanced chassis design, contributes to your peace of mind on every journey. We believe true luxury is feeling completely secure.

Driving innovation, delivering luxury, defining performance.
