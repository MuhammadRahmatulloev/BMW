The All-New BMW M5

Masterpiece of Carering. Symphony of Power.

A Legacy of Uncompromising Performance

The BMW M5 is not just a car; it's a legend forged over decades of racing heritage and relentless innovation. From its origins as a bespoke sports sedan to its current form as a technological tour-de-force, the M5 has consistently redefined what a high-performance luxury vehicle can be. It embodies BMW's commitment to dynamic driving, exquisite craftsmanship, and groundbreaking engineering, pushing the boundaries of what's possible on both road and track.

Unrivaled Performance

TwinPower Turbo V8

At the heart of the M5 beats a potent 4.4-liter TwinPower Turbo V8 engine, delivering thrilling power and an unmistakable roar.

M xDrive System

The M xDrive intelligent all-wheel-drive system ensures optimal traction and agility, with an exclusive 2WD mode for pure driving exhilaration.

Dynamic Handling

Experience unparalleled precision and control with M-specific suspension, steering, and braking systems, finely tuned for dynamic driving.
