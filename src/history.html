BMW: A Century of Driving Innovation

Explore the rich heritage and groundbreaking achievements that have defined BMW's journey from its inception to its vision for the future. Our history is a testament to relentless engineering, iconic design, and a passion for driving pleasure.

The Genesis and Early Years

From its origins in aircraft engine manufacturing, BMW rapidly evolved into a producer of motorcycles and automobiles, demonstrating early engineering prowess and design innovation amidst turbulent global events.

BMW: A Century of Driving Innovation

1916

Founding of BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG is established, initially focused on aircraft engines.

1923

First Motorcycle: R 32

BMW launches its groundbreaking R 32 motorcycle, setting new standards in engineering.

1928

First Automobile: Dixi 3/15

Acquisition of Automobilwerk Eisenach leads to BMW's entry into car manufacturing with the Dixi.

1936

Iconic 328 Roadster

The BMW 328 Roadster is introduced, a legendary sports car celebrated for its advanced design and performance.

Rebuilding and Innovation

Emerging from the post-war challenges, BMW meticulously rebuilt its automotive production, introducing innovative models and laying the foundation for its luxury performance identity.

Key Milestones: 1945-1970

1955

The Isetta Bubble Car

BMW introduces the microcar Isetta, a popular solution for post-war transportation needs.

1962

New Class Sedans

The launch of the New Class sedans marks BMW's resurgence and redefines the sporty sedan segment.

1966

Birth of the 02 Series

The highly successful 02 Series (e.g., 2002) is launched, cementing BMW's reputation for compact sports cars.

1970

Iconic 328 Roadster

The BMW 328 Roadster is introduced, a legendary sports car celebrated for its advanced design and performance.

Performance and Global Expansion

This era saw BMW solidify its position as a global leader in luxury and performance vehicles, introducing legendary model lines and expanding its motorsport division.

Key Milestones: 1970-2000

1972

Founding of BMW M GmbH

The dedicated motorsport division, BMW Motorsport GmbH, is established to create high-performance vehicles.

1975

Launch of the 3 Series

The iconic 3 Series is introduced, becoming BMW's best-selling model and a benchmark for compact executive cars.

1986

First M3 (E30)

The legendary BMW M3 (E30) is released, an instant classic for its racing heritage and road performance.

1999

Introduction of the X5

BMW enters the SUV market with the X5, pioneering the Sports Activity Vehicle (SAV) concept.

The Modern Era and Future

BMW embraces sustainability, electrification, and digital connectivity, redefining driving pleasure for the 21st century with groundbreaking technologies and visionary concepts.

Key Milestones: 2000-Present

2013

BMW i3 & i8 Launch

BMW introduces its 'i' sub-brand with the all-electric i3 city car and the hybrid i8 sports car.

2015

ConnectedDrive Evolution

2021

New Class Vision (Neue Klasse)
